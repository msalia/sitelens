# Plans & Pricing

SiteLens comes in two plans: **Solo**, which is free forever, and **Crew**, a
paid plan for teams running several jobs at once. Every account starts on Solo —
upgrade to Crew whenever you need more projects, more teammates, exports, or DXF
overlays.

## Solo — Free

Solo gives a single surveyor everything needed to tie and visualize one site, at
no cost.

- **1 project**
- **Team up to 6** — 1 admin plus up to 5 members
- **Coordinate-tie** — the 4-parameter Helmert transform with residuals and RMS
- **Coordinate conversion & inspector** — building grid, projected, and lat-long
- **CSV / LandXML point import**
- **3D Cesium visualization**

Solo does not include exports or DXF overlays. If a Crew subscription lapses and
the org is over the Solo caps (more than 1 project, or more than 6 members), the
account becomes read-only until usage is brought back within the caps or the
subscription is renewed.

## Crew — $10/mo or $99/yr

Crew unlocks the full toolkit for a growing team. The yearly plan is the best
value, saving roughly 17% over paying monthly.

Everything in Solo, plus:

- **Unlimited projects**
- **Unlimited admins & members**
- **CSV / LandXML & full project exports**
- **DXF overlays in the 3D view**

## How billing works

Billing is handled securely by **Stripe** — SiteLens never stores card details.
An organization admin can start, change, or cancel a subscription from
**Settings → Billing** inside the app, which opens Stripe Checkout or the Stripe
customer portal.

Prices are shown in USD. Choosing the yearly cadence bills once a year at $99;
the monthly cadence bills $10 each month. You can switch between cadences or
cancel at any time from the billing portal.
